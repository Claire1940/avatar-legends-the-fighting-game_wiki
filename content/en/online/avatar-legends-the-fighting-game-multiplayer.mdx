<Section icon={Shield} title="Netcode and Crossplay: The Technical Foundation">

The backbone of any modern fighting game is its netcode. **Avatar Legends the Fighting Game multiplayer** utilizes a proprietary rollback netcode solution. Unlike traditional delay-based netcode, which pauses the game to wait for data, rollback predicts inputs and "rolls back" the game state if a discrepancy occurs. This results in an online experience that feels as close to local "couch" play as possible, even when playing against opponents hundreds of miles away.

<CardGrid cols={2}>
  <Card title="Rollback Netcode Benefits">
    - **Reduced Input Lag**: Your moves come out instantly regardless of ping.
    - **Visual Consistency**: Smooth animations even during minor packet loss.
    - **Competitive Fairness**: Prevents "lag switching" or unfair advantages.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Crossplay Advantages">
    - **Unified Player Base**: No split communities between PC and Console.
    - **Faster Matchmaking**: More players mean shorter queue times for Ranked.
    - **Play with Friends**: Connect with anyone regardless of their hardware.
  </Card>
</CardGrid>

In addition to the netcode, the inclusion of full crossplay ensures that the community remains vibrant across all platforms. Whether you are on Steam, PlayStation 5, or Xbox Series X|S, you will be part of the same matchmaking pool. This is a massive win for the long-term health of the game, as it prevents the player base from becoming fragmented.
