<Section icon={Target} title="Core Combat Mechanics: Mastering the Flow">

Success in **Avatar Legends the Fighting Game Online** depends on mastering the interaction between the Flow system and Energy management. Unlike traditional fighters that rely heavily on complex directional inputs, this game uses a streamlined control scheme that emphasizes timing, positioning, and resource spending.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Understand the Flow System">
    Flow is the universal movement mechanic. By pressing a single dedicated button, players can dash, slip under projectiles, or transition into character-specific follow-ups. Mastering Flow is essential for bypassing "zoning" characters like Katara.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Manage Energy Points (EP)">
    EP is built by landing attacks and successful blocks. You can spend 1 bar for **Enhanced Special Moves** (adding armor or damage) or save full bars for a **Super Art**, a cinematic finisher that can turn the tide of a round.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Utilize Support Characters">
    Before a match, you select a Support Character (like Appa or Momo). These are not playable but offer assist calls that provide defensive shields, offensive pokes, or movement buffs.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Master the Bending Breakdown">
    Each element has a unique property. Earthbending moves often have "Super Armor," while Airbending moves allow for double-jumping and hovering. Learn these properties to exploit your opponent's elemental weaknesses.
  </Step>
</Steps>

| Mechanic | Purpose | Beginner Tip | Advanced Application |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Flow Dash** | Evasive Movement | Use to dodge projectiles | Cancel normals into Flow for combos |
| **Enhanced Specials** | Power Boost | Use to finish a combo | Use for frame advantage on block |
| **Super Arts** | Cinematic Damage | Only use when it will KO | Use to punish long recovery moves |
| **Support Assist** | Tactical Aid | Use when you are cornered | Use to extend juggle combos |

<Callout type="success" title="Mechanical Depth">
The "Flow" system allows for **Flow Cancels**, which let high-level players stop an attack animation halfway to bait a counter or extend a combo string. This is the key to reaching Diamond rank in the online ladder.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Shield} title="Online Infrastructure: Rollback & Crossplay">

For any modern fighter, the online experience is the most critical component. **Avatar Legends the Fighting Game Online** utilizes a proprietary rollback netcode solution, which predicts player inputs to eliminate the "underwater" feeling of delay-based systems. This ensures that even matches between players in different regions remain responsive.

The game also features full **Cross-play**, meaning Steam, PlayStation, and Xbox players all share the same matchmaking pool. This keeps queue times short and the competitive community unified.

| Feature | Availability | Details |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Rollback Netcode** | Yes | Proprietary system for minimal latency |
| **Cross-platform Play** | Yes | Unified pool for PC, PS5, and Xbox |
| **Ranked Matchmaking** | Yes | Skill-based tiers with seasonal rewards |
| **Spectator Mode** | Yes | Watch live high-level matches in-game |
| **Replay Theater** | Yes | Save and analyze your previous fights |
