<Section icon={Shield} title="Core Combat Mechanics and Armor Systems">

In the current meta of **avatar legends the fighting game toph** stands out as the ultimate "tank" character. Unlike agile fighters who rely on evasion, Toph utilizes her connection to the earth to absorb impact and retaliate with overwhelming force. Her playstyle revolves around the **Armor** system, which dictates how she interacts with incoming projectiles and melee strikes.

| Armor Type | Interaction | Vulnerability |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Standard Armor** | Absorbs one single hit while continuing the move | Super Techniques, Multi-hit moves |
| **Hyper Armor** | Absorbs unlimited hits during the move duration | Super Techniques |
| **Crushing** | Heavy attacks deal damage through blocks | Perfect Parries (if applicable) |
| **Champ's Ring** | Creates a temporary wall on contact | Being thrown by the opponent |

<Callout type="warning" title="Armor Vulnerability">
While Toph's armor makes her nearly unstoppable against standard pokes, **Super Techniques** will break both standard and Hyper Armor instantly. Always monitor your opponent's energy bar before committing to a slow, armored heavy attack.
</Callout>

Toph’s unique **Champ's Ring** mechanic, triggered by Badgermole Sight (EX), introduces a spatial control element rarely seen in other fighters. By creating a physical wall, she can trap opponents and use tackling attacks to cause a "wall bounce," extending her combo potential significantly.

<Section icon={Map} title="Strategic Setup: The Wall Bounce Combo">

The most advanced tactic for Toph players involves the strategic use of the **Champ's Ring** wall. This requires precise energy management and positioning.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Activate Seismic Sense">
    Use **Badgermole's Sight (EX)** (Quarter Circle Back + 2 Attacks) to expand your seismic rings. Ensure the rings make contact with the opponent to trigger the wall creation.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Trap the Opponent">
    With the wall active, the opponent's movement is restricted. Use **Rooted Stance** to bait an attack, utilizing your armor to stay stationary.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Execute the Tackle">
    Once the opponent is near the wall, use a tackling attack or a throw. Knocking them into the earth wall causes a **Wall Bounce**, resetting their hitstun.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Follow-up Combo">
    While the opponent is bouncing off the broken wall, follow up with **Boulder Blitz** or a Super for maximum damage output.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="Wall Duration">
The wall created by Badgermole's Sight (EX) is not permanent. It will disappear if the timer runs out, if the opponent manages to throw Toph, or if it is broken by a wall-bounce interaction.
</Callout>

</Section>
