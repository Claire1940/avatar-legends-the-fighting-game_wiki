<Section icon={Shield} title="Defensive Flow and Reversal Mechanics">

Defending in **Avatar Legends The Fighting Game** is not just about holding back on your controller and waiting for your opponent to finish their turn. Unlike traditional fighting games where you must block entire block strings from characters like Korra, this game introduces dynamic defensive options. The most important mechanic you need to master is **Flow Reversal**.

Flow Reversal is the act of flow canceling while guarding. This mechanic allows you to transition directly from a blocking state into a flow move, effectively interrupting your opponent's pressure. However, you must understand the rules of this system to avoid getting punished.

| Mechanic | Input Condition | Invulnerability | Resource Cost |
|--- |--- |--- |--- |
| **Flow Reversal** | Guarding | None (retains move properties) | Consumes Flow meter |
| **Back Flow Moves** | Cannot be used | N/A | N/A |
| **Air Flow Moves** | Cannot be used (no air blocking) | N/A | N/A |

<Callout type="warning" title="Critical Restriction">
Flow moves assigned to **Back Flow** cannot be used to execute a Flow Reversal. For example, Zuko cannot use his back flow move while guarding because it would allow him to easily jump back and escape pressure instantly.

<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Training Mode Setup">

To master the timing of Flow Reversal and counter-attacks, you must utilize the training mode effectively. Setting up a recording dummy is the best way to build muscle memory.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Access Training Options">
    Open the training menu and navigate to the dummy settings. Set the dummy to Record mode.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Record a Block String">
    Record a standard block string with an aggressive character like Korra. Ensure the string has a small gap or ends in a heavy flow move.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Enable Playback on Block">
    Set the dummy action to "Play on Block" so they immediately execute the recorded string when you attack or interact with them.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Practice the Flow Reversal">
    Hold guard to block the incoming string, then input your chosen Flow Reversal move (such as Aang's evasive air maneuvers) during the gap.
  </Step>
  <Steps num="5" title="Confirm the Punish">
    Practice converting the successful Flow Reversal into a full combo, especially when your back is against the corner.
  </Steps>
</Steps>
