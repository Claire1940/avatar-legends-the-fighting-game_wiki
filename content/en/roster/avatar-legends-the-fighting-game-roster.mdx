The highly anticipated 2D elemental fighter, **Avatar Legends: The Fighting Game**, officially launches on **July 23, 2026**. Unlike generic anime arena fighters, **Avatar Legends: The Fighting Game** is built as a traditional four-button competitive fighter (Light, Medium, Heavy, and Special inputs). The gameplay depth relies heavily on the **Flow Mechanic**, allowing players to dodge incoming attacks, slip beneath projectiles, or chain standard hits into character-specific movement options.

<Section icon={Shield} title="The Support Character System">

Selecting your main fighter is only half the battle. To maximize your efficiency on the **Avatar Legends The Fighting Game roster**, you must pair your character with one of three designated support assists. Support characters are not merely cosmetic; they add active combat abilities, cover matchup weaknesses, and completely reshape how your main fighter plays neutral.

| Support Character | Associated Fighter | Strategic Benefit | Combat Application |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Appa** | Aang | Flow Cancels | Allows Aang to cancel basic techniques for safer pressure |
| **June** | Zuko | Divekick Buffs | Turns Zuko's divekicks into highly oppressive combo starters |
| **Naga** | Korra | State Extension | Extends the duration of Korra's Avatar State until round-end |

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Analyze Your Main's Weakness">
    Identify if your chosen fighter lacks mobility, struggles with zoning, or needs extra defensive options.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Select a Complementary Support">
    Choose a support character that actively fills that gap (e.g., pairing Kyoshi with a double-jump support to boost her mobility).
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Incorporate Support Calls into Combos">
    Practice timing your support assists in Training Mode to extend your blockstrings or launch opponents for massive damage.
  </Step>
</Steps>

If you pre-order the game, you will receive exclusive voting rights starting in late summer 2026 to choose the final DLC character. The candidates include:

- **Amon**: The mysterious Equalist leader who specializes in stripping benders of their powers.

- **King Bumi**: An eccentric Omashu earthbender utilizing unpredictable and wild attacks.
- **Asami Sato**: A non-bender tech genius utilizing Equalist shock gloves and high-tech gadgets.
- **Tenzin**: An airbending master focused on defensive wind barriers and swift, sweeping strikes.
