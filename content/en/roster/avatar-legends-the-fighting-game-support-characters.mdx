<Section icon={Map} title="How the Support Character System Works">

In **avatar legends the fighting game support characters** act as a specialized variation system rather than active strikers. When you select a playable fighter, you choose one of three dedicated supports. These supports do not physically enter the battlefield to execute attacks during active combat; instead, they grant powerful passive buffs, alter the properties of your special moves, or unlock entirely new mobility options. 

This system allows players to customize their favorite benders to suit different playstyles, drawing inspiration from classic fighting game "grooves" or variation mechanics.
