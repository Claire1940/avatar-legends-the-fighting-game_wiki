<Section icon={Target} title="Core Mechanics, Flow, and Training Path">

The combat system is simple to pick up and deep enough to reward lab time. Your biggest early gains will come from movement discipline, support selection, and meter usage. Those three habits decide far more matches than random super attempts.

<Callout type="warning" title="Meter Discipline">
Do not spend Energy the moment you earn it. Save it for confirmed pressure, punish routes, or a clear closing sequence.
</Callout>

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Learn your movement first">
    Use Flow to dodge, reposition, slip under projectiles, and chain into character-specific options. If your movement breaks down, the rest of your game plan becomes much harder.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Choose a support that fills a gap">
    Each fighter can pair with support characters that change your tactical options. Pick support for mobility, pressure, defense, or control rather than for flavor alone.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Practice a small combo route">
    Start with one stable punish route, one anti-air answer, and one corner sequence. That gives you reliable wins before you chase advanced routes.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Add meter and super timing">
    Learn when to use enhanced specials and Super Arts. The strongest use case is usually a confirmed hit, a punish, or a clean round-ending conversion.
  </Step>
</Steps>
