<Section icon={Target} title="The Redemption Gauge System">

The defining characteristic of **avatar legends the fighting game zuko** is the Redemption Gauge. This mechanic reflects Zuko's journey, where failure and struggle eventually lead to mastery. Zuko earns permanent Redemption Levels throughout a match, which persist across rounds.

<Callout type="warning" title="Critical Mechanic">
Redemption is not just a cosmetic tracker; it actively reduces the cost of Flow moves when using Redemption Chains and unlocks new Critical Hit conditions that can turn the tide of a match.
</Callout>

### Earning Redemption
Redemption is earned through specific combat actions. While some are offensive, the most significant gains often come from recovering after being placed in an "Unbalanced" state.

| Action | Redemption Gain |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Entering Unbalanced State** | +8 Points |
| **Honor Pivot** | +2 Points |
| **Blue Spirit Liberation** | +2 Points |
| **Seeking Honor (Critical)** | +4 Points |

### Redemption Levels and Effects
Each level of Redemption requires 8 points to unlock. As Zuko progresses, he gains access to powerful Critical Hit conditions.

| Level | Requirement | Critical Hit Unlocked |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Level 1** | 8 Points | Questioning Allegiance |
| **Level 2** | 16 Points | Confronting Truth |
| **Level 3** | 24 Points | Choosing Integrity |
| **MAX** | 32+ Points | Restoring Balance (Awards 7 Energy) |

</Section>

<Section icon={Swords} title="Comprehensive Move List and Specials">

Zuko’s kit in **avatar legends the fighting game zuko** is a blend of traditional firebending strikes, dual-sword techniques, and high-mobility "Flow Moves." Understanding when to use a standard Special versus an EX version is the difference between a novice and a Fire Lord.

### Command Normals and Flow Moves
These moves form the backbone of Zuko's neutral game. Flow Moves are particularly important for repositioning and maintaining pressure.

- **Igniting Blast**: A quick burst of flame for short-range poking.
- **Honor Pivot**: A tactical repositioning tool that also builds Redemption.
- **Burning Advance**: A forward-moving strike that maintains offensive momentum.
- **Legacy Fade**: A defensive retreat that can be followed up with aerials if using Ran and Shaw.

### Special Techniques
Zuko's specials are designed for both zoning and close-quarters combat. Many can be linked together using the **Redemption Chain** mechanic.

### Supers: The Ultimate Techniques
Zuko has access to two devastating Supers that can end a match instantly.

1.  **Blue Spirit Liberation**: A 4-Energy Super where Zuko dons his mask and dual broadswords. This move is a direct reference to his stealthy alter-ego and offers high speed and unique sword-based hitboxes.
2.  **Ancient Firebending: Zuko Style**: A cinematic display of the Dancing Dragon form, dealing massive damage and showcasing Zuko's mastery of the original firebending style.
