<Section icon={Target} title="How to Start: Roster Progression Guide">

If you are new to fighting games, the 12-character roster might feel overwhelming. Follow these steps to find your main and start climbing the ranked ladders.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Explore the Story Mode">
    The Story Mode, written by Michael Dante DiMartino and Tim Hedrick, forces you to play as multiple characters. This is the best way to get a feel for different bending styles without the pressure of online play.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Utilize Character Lessons">
    Inside Training Mode, navigate to 'Character Lessons.' These are guided tutorials that teach you the specific Flow mechanics and bread-and-butter combos for each fighter.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Test Support Synergies">
    Spend 30 minutes in Training Mode just swapping supports. See which assist feels natural for your offensive rhythm. A support like Naga might help Korra, but Aang might prefer the verticality of Appa.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Jump into Arcade Mode">
    Before hitting Ranked, finish an Arcade run with your chosen character. This will test your ability to handle AI opponents and introduce you to your character's specific narrative ending.
  </Step>
</Steps>
