### Stance Mechanics: Focused Mode vs Firelord Mode

Azula features a dual-stance system that changes her special move properties. Switching between these stances is key to keeping your opponent guessing.

| Stance | Activation Method | Key Benefits | Drawbacks |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Focused Mode** | Hold Down + Utility Button | Instant lightning charges, faster recovery times. | Reduced movement speed while active. |
| **Firelord Mode** | Spend 2 Energy Points | Enhanced damage, double air dashes, chip damage. | Drains Energy continuously over time. |
