<Section icon={Swords} title="Core Gameplay Mechanics & The Chakra Gauge">

Beneath the gorgeous 2D visuals lies a deep, competitive fighting system designed for both newcomers and seasoned veterans. The most critical mechanic revealed in the tutorial trailers is the **Chakra Gauge**. This resource system governs your offensive power, enabling players to turn the tide of battle with enhanced maneuvers. 

Players start each match with a maximum capacity of 5 Chakra points, which can expand to 7 points upon losing a round. Chakra is accumulated by landing special techniques, opening up opponents with overheads or lows, executing throws, and fulfilling character-specific critical hit conditions.

| Mechanic | Chakra Cost | Description & Strategic Use |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Standard EX Move** | 1 Point | Powers up special attacks for extra damage or better frame data. |
| **Invulnerable/Combo EX** | 2 Points | Offers invincibility frames or extends combos for high-damage routes. |
| **Chakra Art** | 4 Points | Highly damaging super moves that can easily close out a round. |
| **True Chakra Art** | 7 Points | Ultimate comeback mechanic utilizing maximum Chakra capacity. |

<Callout type="warning" title="Stance Vulnerability">
While meditating in certain stances (like the Flow Stance) can passively generate Chakra, it leaves you highly vulnerable to devastating critical throws. Use it wisely!
</Callout>
