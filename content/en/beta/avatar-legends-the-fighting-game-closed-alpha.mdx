<Section icon={Target} title="Combat System: Flow and Energy Points">

Mastering the combat in **avatar legends the fighting game closed alpha** requires an understanding of the dual-resource system. Unlike traditional fighters that rely solely on a super meter, this game introduces the "Flow" mechanic, which governs movement and defensive utility, and "Energy Points," which fuel offensive enhancements.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initialize Flow">
    Use the Flow button to dash, air-dash, or slip under projectiles. Every character has a unique Flow-cancel that allows them to transition from a normal attack into a movement option instantly.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Build Energy Points">
    Energy is gained by landing successful hits, blocking efficiently, and utilizing character-specific bending traits. Aggressive play fills the meter faster, but defensive precision rewards you with "Perfect Flow" bonuses.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Execute Enhanced Specials">
    Spend one bar of Energy to perform an Enhanced Special Move. These versions often have faster startup, invincibility frames, or additional hits to extend combos.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Unleash Super Arts">
    When your Energy meter is full, you can trigger a cinematic Super Art. These are high-damage finishers that can turn the tide of a match but leave you vulnerable if blocked.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip" title="Support Synergy">
Don't ignore your support characters. Press the assist button during a Flow-dash to bring in your support for a "Cross-Flow" attack, which can safely close the gap against zoning characters.
</Callout>

</Section>
