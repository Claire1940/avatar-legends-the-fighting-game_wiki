<Section icon={Shield} title="Key Gameplay Mechanics to Master">

Winning matches during the beta requires more than just button mashing. The game is easy to pick up but features deep competitive layers that reward technical execution.

### The Flow System
Flow is the core movement engine of the game. By pressing the dedicated Flow button, you can instantly dash, slip under incoming projectiles, launch into aerial approaches, or cancel active attack animations for extended combos. Mastering Flow cancels is essential for optimal damage.

### Energy Points & Super Arts
As you fight, you build **Energy Points**. This resource can be spent in two ways:
1. **Enhanced Specials**: Strengthens your standard bending moves with extra damage, armor, or better frame data.
2. **Super Arts**: Cinematic, high-damage ultimate attacks that can instantly turn the tide of a match.

### Support Characters
Before a match, you choose one of three support characters (such as Appa) to accompany your fighter. These assists can be called in to break enemy pressure, extend your own combos, or provide defensive coverage while you recover.

| Mechanic | Input / Resource | Tactical Purpose |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Flow Movement** | Dedicated Button | Evasion, animation cancels, high-speed spacing. |
| **Enhanced Specials** | 1 Energy Point | Combo extensions, armored reversals, chip damage. |
| **Super Arts** | Full Energy Bar | Cinematic finishers, massive punish damage. |
| **Support Assist** | Cooldown Timer | Defensive combo breakers, offensive pressure. |
