<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Core Combat Mechanics: Flow and Energy">

Combat in the **avatar legends the fighting game beta** is built on two primary pillars: the Flow system and Energy Points. Unlike traditional fighters that rely solely on frame-perfect inputs, this title emphasizes movement and tactical resource management.

<CardGrid cols={2}>
  <Card title="The Flow System">
    - **Movement**: Allows for instant dashes and slips under projectiles.
    - **Cancels**: Flow can be used to cancel recovery frames of special moves.
    - **Character Specific**: Each bender has unique Flow options (e.g., Air Scooters for Aang).
  </Card>
  <Card title="Energy Points (EP)">
    - **Accumulation**: Built by landing attacks and successful blocks.
    - **Enhanced Specials**: Spend 1 bar of EP to add properties like armor or extra hits.
    - **Super Arts**: Spend full meter for a cinematic, high-damage finisher.
  </Card>
</CardGrid>

<Callout type="success" title="Advanced Mechanic: Bending Cancels">
Experienced players can use Flow to cancel a bending projectile into a physical dash. This is the primary way to "reset" your pressure and keep opponents guessing in the corner.
</Callout>

Each bending style also dictates how a character interacts with the environment. Earthbenders can create temporary pillars that act as physical barriers, while Waterbenders can manipulate the trajectory of their projectiles mid-flight. These nuances are fully available for testing in the beta's Training and Versus modes.
