<Section icon={Target} title="Mastering the Flow: Beginner Steps">

To succeed in *Avatar Legends: The Fighting Game*, you must master the **Flow Mechanic**. This system governs how characters move, dodge, and transition between elemental strikes. Unlike standard fighters where movement can feel rigid, the Flow system allows for "slipping" under projectiles and launching into air-to-ground combos with a single input.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Select Your Main">
    Choose a character whose bending style matches your preferred playstyle. Aang is great for those who love movement, while Toph suits players who prefer a "tanky" defensive approach.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Learn Flow Cancels">
    Use the Flow button to cancel the recovery of your special moves. This allows you to stay safe on block or extend a combo that would otherwise end.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Manage Energy Points">
    Energy builds as you land hits and block successfully. Spend it on **Enhanced Special Moves** for extra damage or save it for a cinematic **Super Art** to finish the round.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Utilize Supports">
    Don't forget your support character. Call them during a combo to keep the opponent in the air, or use them defensively to push a rushing opponent away.
  </Step>
</Steps>
