Participating in the vote requires a verified digital pre-order of the game on any supported platform, including Steam, PlayStation 5, Xbox Series X|S, or Nintendo Switch. The voting system is designed to reward early adopters and prevent botting.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Pre-Order the Game">
    Purchase either the Standard Edition ($29.99) or the Digital Deluxe Edition ($49.99) on your preferred storefront before the July 23, 2026 launch.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Register Your Account">
    Link your platform account (Steam, PSN, or Xbox Live) to the official [Avatar Fighters Website](https://avatarfighters.com/) to verify your pre-order status.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Wait for Late Summer">
    Keep an eye on your registered email or the official X / Twitter account (@avatar_fighters) for the official voting portal launch in late summer 2026.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Cast Your Ballot">
    Log into the portal, select your preferred character from the five candidates, and submit your vote. Each verified pre-order is limited to one vote.
  </Step>
</Steps>
