<Section icon={Shield} title="Combat Mechanics & Meter Management">

Combat revolves around precise movement, support assists, and managing your **Energy Points** meter. Knowing when to spend resources can mean the difference between a clutch comeback and a swift defeat.

- **Flow Mechanic**: The universal movement system allowing quick dashes, air recoveries, and projectile evasion.
- **Energy Points**: Built by landing attacks and executing perfect blocks; spent on enhanced specials and cinematic Super Arts.
- **Support Characters**: Off-screen assists that offer tactical utility, space control, or combo-breaking bursts.
- **Enhanced Specials**: Upgraded versions of your standard bending moves that add invincibility, extra damage, or faster startup.

| Mechanic | Resource Cost | Input Difficulty | Best Tactical Use |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Flow Dash** | None | Low | Escaping corner pressure and closing gaps |
| **Enhanced Special** | 1 Energy Bar | Medium | Extending combos or punishing unsafe moves |
| **Super Art** | Full Energy Meter | High | Closing out rounds and maximizing damage |
| **Support Assist** | Cooldown Timer | Low | Interrupting enemy pressure or zoning |
