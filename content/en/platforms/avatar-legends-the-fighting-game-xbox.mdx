<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Combat Mechanics: Flow and Energy Systems">

To succeed in **avatar legends the fighting game xbox**, players must look beyond simple button mashing. The combat engine is built on two primary pillars: the **Flow Mechanic** and **Energy Points**. Flow is a dedicated movement and defensive system that allows for slips, dashes, and character-specific cancels. Mastering Flow is the difference between being trapped in a corner and executing a perfect counter-attack.

**Energy Points** function as the game's meter system. Players build Energy by landing attacks, successfully blocking, and using Flow effectively. This meter can be spent on three types of actions:
1. **Enhanced Specials:** More powerful versions of base moves with added properties like armor or faster startup.
2. **Super Arts:** Cinematic finishers that deal massive damage and can turn the tide of a round.
3. **Support Calls:** Utilizing your chosen support character to assist in battle.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Learn Your Flow">
    Practice the Flow input to understand your character's unique evasion. Some characters slip under projectiles, while others launch into the air.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Manage Energy Points">
    Don't waste meter on Enhanced Specials if they won't hit. Save at least one bar for defensive support calls if you are under heavy pressure.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Synergize with Supports">
    Choose a support that covers your character's weaknesses. If you play a slow character like Toph, pick a support that offers mobility or projectile protection.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Confirm into Super Arts">
    Only trigger Super Arts during guaranteed combo windows or as a hard punish for an opponent's whiffed big move.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Meter Management">
Emptying your Energy meter completely leaves you vulnerable to "Chi Block" states where your defensive options are limited for a short duration.
</Callout>

</Section>
