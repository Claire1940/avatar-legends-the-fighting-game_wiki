<Section icon={Shield} title="Combat Mechanics and Online Systems">

Beneath its beautifully animated exterior, the game is built to accommodate both casual fans and hardcore competitive players. The core combat revolves around two major systems: **Flow** and **Energy Points**.

- **The Flow Mechanic**: This high-speed movement option lets players dash, evade, and slip under incoming projectiles with ease. Every character has unique Flow pathways, which can be canceled into special attacks or used to launch opponents into aerial juggle combos.
- **Energy Points**: Built up by executing successful blocks, landing clean hits, and utilizing smart movement. These points can be spent to perform **Enhanced Special Moves** or saved up to unleash cinematic, round-ending **Super Arts**.

### Online Infrastructure
For competitive players, online integrity is paramount. The developers have integrated industry-standard systems to ensure lag-free matches across the globe:

- **Proprietary Rollback Netcode**: Built from the ground up to prevent visual stuttering and input delay during high-level matches.
- **Full Crossplay Support**: Players on Nintendo Switch 2, PS5, Xbox Series X|S, and PC can seamlessly matchmake against one another.
- **Ranked & Casual Modes**: Climb the competitive ladder or enjoy stress-free fights in custom lobbies.
- **Spectator & Replay Suite**: Watch ongoing tournament lobbies or review your personal match replays to study frame data and refine your strategies.
